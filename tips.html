Beginner: Tips & Tricks!

- Intan Shahirah

You can do doodle everywhere including during the meeting and in a class as long as you relax and let your hand to work for it. You will see what are you are thinking right at that moment, it might be an original, funny, sad, or even very artistic skills doodle. The first think to doodle is by having a simple tool such as pencil, ball pen, marker and highlighter. Next is by doodle whatever pops into your head. You can just keep on doodle and do the repetition until you get the other idea. You just can mix everything into one drawing. Maybe you can doodle from your own name, you bestfriend, everything around you, flowers, cloud, animals. Other than that, you can do your own doodle journal. You can see the other perspective of your own doodle or drawing itself. It might be you get your own inspiration from your travel, hobbies and many more. For the beginner, it is okay to make mistakes. If you are not confident enough, you can use pencil and eraser. After you get expert, then you can use pen or anything else to just cover your mistake and make it a new doodle drawing.
